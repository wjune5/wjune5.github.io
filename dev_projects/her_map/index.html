Abstract

Her Map is a map-based platform designed to help women easily discover female-friendly businesses and personal service providers. Providers can register and submit their information, which is displayed on an interactive map. Users can browse, filter, and select services based on location and category, creating a safer and more supportive environment for women to access what they need.
