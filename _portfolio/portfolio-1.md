This application features a categorized question management system.
<br/>
Use OpenCV to cut all questions from books or papers.
<br/><img src='/images/tiji_home.jpg'>
<br/><img src='/images/tiji_b_home.jpg'>
<br/>
Questions are organized by subjects, and some subjects include nested subcategories, providing a clear and hierarchical structure. Users can easily add, edit, or delete categories and subcategories through an intuitive interface.
<br/><img src='/images/tiji_b_subjects.jpg'>


It was developed when i was preparing for Graduate Entrance Exam on 2023.
